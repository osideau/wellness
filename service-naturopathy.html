— About this service

Find the root.
Treat the cause.

Naturopathic medicine is holistic, evidence-informed medical care focused on identifying and addressing the underlying causes of illness — not just managing symptoms.

Our Naturopathic Doctors provide comprehensive assessments and individualized treatment plans. Drawing on clinical nutrition, botanical medicine, and lifestyle counselling, they work alongside your existing care team to support long-term health from the inside out.

  • —Clinical nutrition
  • —Botanical medicine
  • —Lifestyle counselling
  • —Supplementation & hormone support
  • —Lab testing & functional diagnostics
— What to expect

Your first visit,
step by step.

01

Comprehensive intake

Your first visit is 60–75 minutes. Your ND will review your full health history — from symptoms and lifestyle to sleep, stress, diet, and family history — to see the full picture.

02

Lab testing & assessment

Where relevant, your ND may order lab work to identify nutritional deficiencies, hormonal imbalances, or other markers underlying your symptoms.

03

Individualized treatment plan

You'll leave with a clear, personalized plan covering dietary changes, supplementation, botanical medicine, and lifestyle adjustments — with priorities ordered for your life.

04

Follow-up care

Regular follow-up appointments track your progress, adjust the plan, and review lab results. Your ND works alongside your existing healthcare providers.
