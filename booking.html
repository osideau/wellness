A few notes on
your first visit.

i Arrive early
First appointments include a short intake conversation. A few minutes early lets us begin on time.
ii Direct billing
We direct bill most major extended health providers. Bring your insurance details to your first visit.
iii Cancellation
We ask for at least 24 hours notice for cancellations, so the time can go to someone who needs it.
iv Need help?
If you are not sure which practitioner or treatment is right, reach out and we will help.
