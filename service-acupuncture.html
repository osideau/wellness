— About this service

Ancient practice,
modern evidence.

Acupuncture is a safe, evidence-supported therapy that stimulates the body's natural healing response — integrating Traditional Chinese Medicine principles with modern clinical practice.

By targeting specific points along the body's meridian pathways, acupuncture helps regulate the nervous system, reduce inflammation, and support the body's own capacity to heal. It's effective for a wide range of conditions, from chronic pain and stress to hormonal balance and sleep.

— What to expect

Your first visit,
step by step.

01

Health history

Your first acupuncture session begins with a detailed health history. Your practitioner will ask about your current concerns, lifestyle, sleep, digestion, and more — building a full picture of your constitution.

02

Diagnosis

Using pulse and tongue diagnosis alongside your history, your practitioner will identify patterns and imbalances to guide the treatment plan.

03

Treatment

Fine, sterile needles are placed at specific points. Most patients find it deeply relaxing. You'll rest comfortably for 20–30 minutes while the treatment takes effect.

04

After care

Your practitioner will recommend a treatment schedule and any lifestyle or dietary adjustments to support the work between sessions.
