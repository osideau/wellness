— The team Eight therapists, one acupuncturist.

Liza Pinili

Registered Massage Therapist

A 2022 graduate of the Vancouver College of Massage Therapy, Liza treats orthopedic sprains and strains, repetitive strain injuries, and post-surgical rehabilitation. Her background as an ICBC rehabilitation assistant informs a patient-centred approach to motor-vehicle-accident recovery, with an emphasis on education and home care. Modalities include Swedish, myofascial release, trigger point release, PNF, and joint mobilization.

Please note: Liza is severely allergic to coconut — kindly avoid coconut-based products before your visit.

Melanie Gunther

Registered Massage Therapist

With 25 years in the natural health industry, Melanie is the founder of the award-winning supplement line Living Alchemy, a Registered Holistic Nutritionist, and an RMT since 2021 with a Bachelor's degree in Health Science. Her hands-on and tool-assisted techniques restore whole-body balance, release tension, and restore range of motion.

Jerry Ju

Registered Massage Therapist

A calm, easygoing graduate of the Vancouver College of Massage Therapy, Jerry takes a holistic, client-centred approach to sports injuries, stress and anxiety, and post-surgery rehabilitation. His services include Swedish massage, deep tissue, and lymphatic drainage therapy, supported by his own practice of healthy living and meditation.

Cayley Malo

Registered Massage Therapist

A 2017 graduate of Langara College's RMT program with 3,200 hours of training, Cayley has treated repetitive strain injuries, postural issues, sports injuries, back pain, headaches, and neurological conditions. She tailors each treatment with myofascial release, joint mobilizations, trigger point release, Swedish, and stretching to help patients return to the activities they love.

Jose Chacara Sanchez

Registered Massage Therapist

A graduate of the Canadian College of Massage and Hydrotherapy in Toronto, Jose focuses on lower back, shoulder, and knee pain, and chronic conditions such as MS, fibromyalgia, carpal tunnel syndrome, and frozen shoulder. His work spans sports, deep tissue, Swedish, chronic-pain, relaxation, and prenatal massage, grounded in anatomy and current research.

Summer Lee

Registered Massage Therapist

Summer immigrated to Canada from Seoul over 20 years ago and completed her RMT training at Bryan College of Massage Therapy in Toronto in 2021. She also holds a Bachelor's degree in Gerontology from McMaster University. Alongside Swedish massage, she incorporates trigger point release, myofascial release, and PNF for comprehensive, evidence-informed treatment.

Anthony Lau

Registered Acupuncturist

A Registered Acupuncturist who graduated with Distinction from Kwantlen Polytechnic University, Anthony apprenticed under respected Traditional Chinese Medicine masters. He manages sports injuries, trigeminal neuralgia, post-shingles nerve pain, insomnia, and digestive disorders, with expertise in cupping, moxibustion, gua sha, auricular therapy, and tuina.
