Liza Pinili

Registered Massage Therapist

A 2022 graduate of the Vancouver College of Massage Therapy, Liza provides evidence-informed treatment for orthopedic sprains and strains, repetitive strain injuries, and post-surgical rehabilitation. Her experience as an ICBC rehabilitation assistant supports a patient-centred approach to motor-vehicle-accident recovery, with a strong emphasis on education, movement, and effective home-care strategies.

As the clinic owner, Liza is committed to fostering a welcoming, supportive environment for both clients and practitioners. Even with her leadership role, she remains a therapist at heart — deeply dedicated to hands-on care and to helping clients feel understood, empowered, and well supported in their recovery.

She incorporates a range of therapeutic modalities into her practice, including Swedish massage, myofascial release, trigger point therapy, proprioceptive neuromuscular facilitation (PNF), and joint mobilization, tailoring each session to meet individual needs and support long-term functional wellbeing.

Please note: Liza is severely allergic to coconut — kindly avoid coconut-based products before your visit.

Anthony Lau

Registered Acupuncturist

Anthony Lau is a Registered Acupuncturist who graduated with Distinction from Kwantlen Polytechnic University (KPU). During his training, he apprenticed under several respected Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) masters, gaining advanced clinical skills and a deep understanding of classical TCM practice. He has extensive experience treating sports injuries, trigeminal neuralgia, post-shingles nerve pain (PHN), insomnia, and digestive disorders.

Anthony is skilled in a wide range of TCM modalities, including diverse cupping techniques, moxibustion, scraping therapy (Gua sha), auricular therapy, Tuina massage, and pediatric meridian massage. He takes a gentle, patient-centred approach, listening closely to each individual's needs and designing customized acupuncture-based treatment plans that support long-term health and recovery.

Joshua Kim

Registered Massage Therapist

Joshua Kim is a Registered Massage Therapist licensed with the CMTBC. He holds a BSc in Life Science from UBC — a foundation in anatomy, physiology, and health sciences that informs his clinical approach. He completed comprehensive massage therapy training and graduated with honours, deepening his commitment to evidence-informed and client-centred care.

Prior to becoming an RMT, Joshua worked in public service roles within government and national agencies, shaping values of professionalism, compassion, and genuine care for others.

He provides deep tissue massage, myofascial release, fascial stretch therapy, and joint care — tailored to chronic pain, injury recovery, and tension-related concerns — alongside personalized home care and lifestyle strategies.

Having experienced chronic pain himself, Joshua brings empathy and insight to each session, creating a calm, respectful space where clients feel understood and supported. Outside the clinic, he enjoys time with his wife and three young children — a grounded, family-oriented perspective that reflects the heart of Isorropia Wellness Clinic.
