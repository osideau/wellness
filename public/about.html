— The Practice

A small
practice, with
uncommon care.

Liza & Co. began with a simple frustration: that wellness, in most clinics, had become a transaction. We are building something quieter, slower, and considerably more careful.

— The treatment space No. 02
— Beginning

A clinic
of one,
becoming
a clinic of many.

The practice began with a single registered massage therapist and a refusal. A refusal to book in fifteen minute slots. A refusal to begin a treatment without first beginning a conversation.

We wanted to build the kind of clinic we would want for the people we love. Longer appointments. Continuity. Treatment plans built around the person, not the schedule.

As we grow into a multi-disciplinary practice, that founding idea has only sharpened.

We chose to be small,
so the work could be large.

— Principles

How we practice.

i Listen first
First appointments begin with a conversation. What brought you in. What has worked. What has not.
ii Slower appointments
Treatments take the time they need. Proper time to settle in, proper time to come back.
iii Long-term care
Most of our work is with people we see regularly. We build treatment plans for the arc, not the moment.
iv Disciplines together
Our practitioners speak with one another. Your care does not get fragmented across departments.

"The clinic we are building is the clinic we wanted to find ourselves."

Liza Pinili, Founder & RMT
